More About the Designer //

Paola is an experienced interior architect with a deep passion for creating both residential and commercial spaces. Holding a master's degree in industrial design, she brings a comprehensive understanding of both the aesthetic and functional dimensions of interior architecture. Her attentive approach and strong focus on detail help her deliver work that is both harmonious and visually compelling.

Paola's background in project management complements her design skills, allowing her to plan and execute projects efficiently while ensuring smooth coordination and timely delivery. She is committed to truly understanding her clients' needs and transforming their visions into designs that surpass expectations.

Believing in the power of collaboration, Paola works closely with architects and designers, drawing on their combined expertise to craft cohesive and innovative spaces. This collaborative process not only enriches her projects but also fosters creativity and drives continuous improvement in her work.

bars and eateries

JIVE / COCKTAIL BAR

Type: Cocktail Bar / Location: Gemmayze Beirut, Lebanon / Area: ~60 sqm / Completed in 2022

Jive is an all-day bar and hangout located in the vibrant street of Gemmayzeh. It presents a re-interpretation of the street disco, drawing inspiration from the culture of the seventies. The collaboration with designer and co-owner, Jihane Fares, ensured that every detail of the design is in complete synchrony with the Jive identity. Throughout the space, a balance of light and color highlights different materials and settings, providing a unique experience both during the day and in the absence of daylight.

16 MM / COCKTAIL BAR

Type: Resto-Bar / Location: Gemmayze Beirut, Lebanon / Area: ~60 sqm / Completed in 2019

Step into a movie-inspired bar, where cinematic dreams come alive within its enchanting interior. The walls are adorned with vintage film posters, immortalizing beloved classics. Soft, dimmed lights evoke a nostalgic ambiance, reminiscent of a bygone era. Plush velvet curtains drape gracefully, adding an air of old Hollywood glamour.

BYBLOS / RESTAURANT

Type: Eatery / Location: Byblos, Lebanon / Indoor: ~180 sqm, Outdoor: ~200 sqm / Completed in 2018

Nestled within the ancient walls of the historic city of Byblos lies this Lebanese fusion restaurant. The interior exudes a timeless charm, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the region. Weathered stone walls, adorned with rustic tapestries and archways, transport patrons to another era. Soft light dances upon wooden tables, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere.

STEAK HOUSE / RESTAURANT

Type: Eatery / Location: Doha, Qatar / Indoor Area: 180 sqm / Completed in 2023

Enter a steakhouse restaurant where industrial elements and culinary mastery unite. The interior showcases a captivating fusion of raw materials, with corten steel accents and exposed brick walls lending an urban and rugged charm. The centerpiece is a visible kitchen, bustling with skilled chefs crafting sizzling cuts of meat to perfection.

ROOFTOP BAR

Type: Nightclub / Location: Dbaye, Lebanon / Area: ~450 sqm / Completed in 2017

This rooftop nightclub is an extraordinary venue that combines bold colors, geometric forms, zigzag patterns, and lush greenery. Its rectangular layout with various levels ensures excellent visibility towards the central bar and DJ booth from all standing points. The interior exudes a vibrant and energetic atmosphere, with vivid hues of electric blue, pink, and fiery orange.

ORENDA COFFEE SHOP

SUNSET BEACH BAR

Type: Beach Bar / Location: Batroun, Lebanon / Area: ~50 sqm / Completed in 2019

Nestled along the Mediterranean sea, a boho beach bar invites visitors into a haven of relaxed elegance. Handmade lighting fixtures illuminate the space, casting a warm, ethereal glow on the eclectic decor. Driftwood and seashells adorn the walls, while macrame hangings sway gently in the ocean breeze. stores, offices and commercial spaces

EVENT EXPERIENCES / PARIS 2024

Type: Exhibition / Location: Palais de Tokyo, Paris France / Area: 15,000 sqm / Completed in 2024

Part of the Paris 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games

Clubhouse24, a first-of-its-kind venue, was part of the official Paris 2024 Olympics Hospitality Venues. The modern art museum was transformed into an immersive sports experience, covering 15,000 sqm of sports, arts, and entertainment where culture, creativity, and athleticism converged.

SALON 24 / PARIS 2024

Type: Exhibition / Location: Maison de l'Amerique latine, Paris France / Area: 3,000 sqm / Completed in 2024

Part of the Paris 2024 Olympic and Paralympic Games

Thematically designed as a retrospective of the historic Parisian art salons, Salon 24 is a hospitality experience inspired by the visionary founder of the modern Olympics, Pierre de Coubertin. Following in Coubertin's footsteps through the Paris Olympic Games from 1900 through present day, Salon 24 celebrates the evolution of 124 years of sport, art, and culture.

OLYMPISM MADE VISIBLE / PARIS 2024

Type: Photography Exhibition / Location: Maison de l'Amerique latine, Paris France / Area: 620 sqm / Completed in 2024

We had the pleasure of assisting in the layout and design of artist panels for an exhibition featuring over 115 photographs from the Olympism Made Visible initiative. Twelve artists depicted the practice of sport from diverse regions, emphasizing its role in empowering individuals and communities.

POP UP STORE

Type: Retail Space / Area: ~80 sqm / Completed in 2019

The store features a concentric layout and a predominantly pastel pink color palette. Custom-made furniture and exhibition stands add uniqueness and enhance the overall aesthetic appeal. Soft and delicate hues of pink, such as blush, baby pink, and rose quartz, dominate the store's color palette, creating a calming and inviting atmosphere.

CORPORATE OFFICES

Type: Offices / Area: ~2,000 sqm / Completed in 2024

For the headquarters of this private holding company, a lavish art deco approach with a modern touch is applied. Moroccan green marble, glass, stone, geometric forms, and wood cladding come together to sculpt the space. The design's philosophy lies behind bringing forward a balanced game of contrasts: light and dark, pastel and vivid colors, bold and timid materials, transparency and privacy.

CORPORATE OFFICES

Type: Offices / Area: ~1,500 sqm / Completed in 2019

Polished bottocino marble flooring, herringbone patterns, modern furniture, delicate lighting are characteristics that shape these corporate offices. The reigning ambiance is classical modernized practicality. Recurring materials are used in the entrance, meeting room and waiting room, reflecting the company's identity with the "msharabiya" pattern extracted from the company's logo.
